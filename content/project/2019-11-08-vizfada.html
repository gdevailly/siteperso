VizFaDa, for visualisation of FAANG data, aims at adding data visualisations to the FAANG data portal. It was funded by the Agence Nationale de la Recherche in 2019 on the Flash - Open Science call, and will start in January 2020 for two years.

The proposal of the project is available to here : vizfada_proposal.pdf. It is inspired by two previous web application exploring human and model organisms data: heat*seq for correlation heatmaps between experiments, and PEREpigenomics for exploring associations between epigenetic marks and transcription.
